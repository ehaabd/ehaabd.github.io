Tic Tac Toe Squared

A new version of the classic Tic-Tac-Toe!

Score by playing in the smaller boxes (you start in the center one). Based on the tile that you play on, you must then play in the field of the corresponding tile on the large field. When you score in a small field, the large box becomes yours. When X scores, it turns blue, and when O scores, it turns yellow. If both score, it becomes green. If a player is a directed to a full small box, they will be redirected to the first playable box from the top left. First person to have 3 boxes in a row wins. If at any point 3 boxes in a row turn green, it will end in a draw.
