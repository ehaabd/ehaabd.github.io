LWSD Volunteer Hub

This is a site that is great for both students and educators. Connect with teachers and students everywhere to plan out your volunteering, submit forms, and log hours. Click here to go through a tutorial on the site or continue to your account page!

Let's Go!

"Connecting students and educators to create a better learning and work environment."

Connect to Teachers and Students

Once you create an account you can put up volunteer opportunities as a teacher or educator, and a student can access and select these opportunities with ease. Sign up to learn more!

Submit Required Forms

LWSD requires students to submit forms in order to participate in volunteering - fill out and submit forms, as well as recieve them, directly through the site.

Log Hours

Whenever a volunteer activity is completed, your hours will automatically be logged and a teacher or educator can sign off on them
